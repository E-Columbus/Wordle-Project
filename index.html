HOW TO PLAY WORDLE?

1 - You win by guessing the word of the day (five letters and in English).

2 - You only have six tries though, write one complete word per line and hit enter to check your answer:

Green - You guessed the correct letter and its position within the word.

Yellow - You guessed a letter, but you typed it in the wrong position. (could be one you already guessed or a second one within the word)

Red - The letter isn't part of the word.
